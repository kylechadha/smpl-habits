Design Notes

  • Overflow (100%+): Blue glow effect on battery. "Overcharged" state with pulsing spark animation.
  • Good (60-100%): Green fill. Battery is charged and healthy.
  • Warning (30-59%): Amber fill. Battery running low, needs attention.
  • Critical (0-29%): Red fill. Battery nearly empty, habit at risk.
  • Swipe to log: Swipe right reveals green checkmark. Row snaps back after logging. Arrow hint on right edge suggests swipeability.
  • Logged state: Green "Done" badge appears. Subtle green gradient on left edge of card. No swipe hint arrow.
  • Typography: Inter font. 28px title, 15px habit names, 11px metadata.
  • Battery size: 32x56px. Compact but clearly readable. Cap adds familiar battery silhouette.
