Design Notes

  • Tap Anywhere to Log: The entire card is tappable. Card briefly pulses on tap for haptic-like feedback. Tap again to undo.
  • Health-Based Card Tinting:
    Excellent (100%+): Subtle green tint
    Good (60-99%): Light green tint
    Warning (30-59%): Amber/orange tint
    Critical (0-29%): Red tint
  • Logged State: Green background with checkmark badge in top-right corner. Clear visual distinction from unlogged habits.
  • Weekly Progress Dots: For X/week habits, dots show progress this week. Filled = completed, empty = remaining.
  • Health Percentage: Small badge showing exact health %. Color-coded to match state.
  • Long-Press for Edit: Hint at bottom reminds users they can long-press to access edit/delete.
  • Typography: Inter font. 34px title, 17px habit names, 13px metadata.
  • FAB: Vibrant blue with prominent shadow. Always visible for adding new habits.

Interaction Flow

1. User sees habits sorted by manual order
2. Tap any card to log for today (pulse animation)
3. Card changes to logged state (green background + checkmark)
4. Health % increases, weekly dots update if applicable
5. Tap logged card again to undo
6. Long-press any card to edit name, frequency, or delete
