Design Notes

  • Overflow (100%+): Teal bar fills completely with dot indicator. Card gets light teal background when logged.
  • Good (60-100%): Teal progress bar. Habit is healthy.
  • Warning (30-59%): Amber bar signals attention needed.
  • Critical (0-29%): Red bar warns of imminent failure.
  • Checkbox: Right-aligned for easy thumb reach. Tap to log, tap again to undo.
  • Typography: Inter font. Large 32px title, 16px habit names, 12px metadata.
  • Spacing: 16px card padding, 8px gap between cards, generous whitespace.
  • FAB: Floating action button for adding new habits. Always visible.
