Backfill Interaction Flow

1

Default View

User sees habit list with health bars and today's checkbox. Subtle left-arrow hint suggests swipe.

2

Swipe Left

Swipe reveals 7-day backfill row below the habit. Shows last 7 days with date numbers and checkboxes.

3

Toggle Past Days

Tap any checkbox to log/unlog that day. Today is highlighted and slightly larger for emphasis.

4

Dismiss

Swipe right, tap chevron, or tap elsewhere to collapse the backfill row back to default view.

Design Notes

  • 2-Action Backfill: Swipe left + tap day. No navigation to other screens required.
  • Overflow (100%+): Full teal bar with dot indicator. Shows banked health buffer.
  • Good (60-100%): Teal bar. Habit is healthy.
  • Warning (30-59%): Amber bar signals attention needed.
  • Critical (0-29%): Red bar warns of imminent failure.
  • Swipe Hint: Subtle left chevron near checkbox hints at swipe gesture. Not intrusive.
  • 7-Day Window: Most recent 7 days shown. Today is larger/highlighted. Date numbers for clarity.
  • Collapse: Right chevron in backfill row, swipe right, or tap outside to dismiss.
  • Checkbox Position: Today's checkbox stays right-aligned for easy thumb reach.
  • Backfill Row: Appears below habit content with subtle gray background for visual separation.
